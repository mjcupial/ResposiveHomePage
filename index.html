Thesis title:
"Comparison of the effect of temperature on the parameters of optoelectronics MSM photodetector constructed on different types of AIIIBV layers"
The purpose of this work was to dermine the dependence of temperature effect on the MSM photodetector parameters contructed on different types of AIIIBV layers.
There are three basic parts of the diploma thesis: a theoretical analysis including the description of light absorption and AIIIBV materials, the temperature influence on specific semiconductors parameters and Schottky barrier height of metal - semiconductor junction. The first part of diploma thesis include also the description of the MSM photodetector along with its basic parameters. The next part describes the measurement setup, the measurement methodology and the tested structures. The final part of the work is the presentation, analysis and interpretation of the obtained measurements and results.

Thesis title:
"Design of technology of ohmic contacts to n-type InGaAs layers"
The contact pads are performed from various metals with appropriate properties. When designing and producing a device, a series of measurements, experiments should be made. The question is how to improve specific parameters. The optimal implementation of the technological process of creating ohmic contacts is time-consuming and difficult to perform. Improving one parameter it is possible to deteriorate another one. In the first part of diploma thesis InGaAs semiconductor is described. Its material properties and applications in today's electronics. Next part describes electrical parameters of ohmic contacts with short description of how to measure it. The definition of the specific contact resistivity ρc and the TLM (Transmission Line Measurement) method of measuring the test structures will be given. Next chapter presents the method of test structure preparation and how technical parameters (metal type, temperature, time of the heating process) influence on the quality of contact. The final part of the work is the presentation, analysis and interpretation of the obtained measurements and results.
